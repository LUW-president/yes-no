# Phase 6 Engagement Question Pack v1 (MVP)

Goal: maximize first-session engagement with practical, immediate, low-friction questions.

## Primary funnel (food-first)
1. Is there something you want right now?
2. Is it related to food or drink?
3. Do you want it in the next 30 minutes?
4. Would ordering help right now?
5. Would you like me to open Uber Eats?

## Fallback funnel (calm/music)
1. Do you want to feel calmer right now?
2. Would music help right now?
3. Would you like me to play something familiar?

## Why this works
- Practical immediacy
- Clear yes/no momentum
- Obvious next action
- Lower cognitive load than abstract themes

## Scope notes
- Deterministic and single-session compatible
- No decision-engine change in this doc phase
