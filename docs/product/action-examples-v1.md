# Action Examples v1

Purpose: reference examples of populated action templates.

## Example variables
- favorite_food = Chinese
- favorite_music = Elvis
- favorite_podcast = Philosophy
- massage_provider = Rosa
- hospital = Mount Sinai

## Example proposals
- Would you like me to order your usual Chinese food?
- Would you like me to play Elvis?
- Would you like the latest philosophy podcast?
- Would you like me to book Rosa your masseuse?
- Do you want me to contact your care team at Mount Sinai?
- Would you like me to find a TaskRabbit to fix that hole in the wall?

## Notes
- Examples are illustrative and deterministic.
- No behavior changes are introduced in this phase.
