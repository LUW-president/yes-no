# First Pilot Packet v0 — YES/NO

## purpose
Provide a controlled, first-review packet for one external reviewer to understand YES/NO clearly and give focused early feedback.

## who this packet is for
- one carefully selected reviewer (design, product, or technical)
- trusted early collaborator who can give thoughtful signal, not broad market validation

## disclosure points
- prototype only
- no production deployment
- gesture recognition is prototype-level
- roadmap items are not shipped
- this is controlled limited sharing, not public launch

## what feedback we want
- was the product thesis clear in the first minute?
- did the YES/NO interaction feel intuitive?
- what felt most powerful?
- what felt confusing or fragile?
- did limitations feel credible and clearly stated?

## what we are not asking yet
- no requests for broad feature expansion
- no pricing or GTM validation asks
- no large roadmap commitments
- no production-readiness judgments beyond prototype context
